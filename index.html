AI Contest

Meet the AI Contest Winners!

Winner 1st Place

Adeh Mirzakhanian

Call Center Recordings AI Processor

A system that utilizes the OpenAI Wisper library to automate the transcription of call recordings from any language to any other language. Additionally, it incorporates an in-house AI model to remove personally identifiable information (PII) from both the transcribed and original versions. The system also categorizes and summarizes the conversations, assesses customer satisfaction with the service, and assigns the records to customers' history, leveraging all the capabilities of OpenAI's APIs. Furthermore, it offers solutions for call center data analysis, optimization, and advanced searching, enabling efficient and effective management of call center operations.

Winner 2nd Place

Yifan Yang

Customized Chatbot

Proposed to develop a customized Chatbot specifically designed for Curacao customers by utilizing AI tools that can integrate Curacao's unique data with robust language models. This Chatbot will help in various areas such as credit applications, customer service, website navigation, and product selection. Moreover, it can be further tailored with additional data to support Curacao employees in tasks like customer service, collection, training, and other relevant areas.

Winner 3rd Place

Edith Karina Cruz

AI-powered virtual assistant

Everyday, users encounter errors related to account information while checking out, leading to abandoned shopping carts. These errors require customer service intervention for resolution. However, when customer service isn't available, customers tend to delay or abandon purchases. This situation impacts key service level KPIs. Most customers, upon encountering an error, abandon their cart if they can't resolve the issue independently. Analysis of user behavior shows that customers prefer to edit personal information on their own but end up abandoning the cart if the error persists. To address this, we propose leveraging ChatGPT, an AI-powered virtual assistant, to guide customers in updating their information when errors occur. This approach reduces the need for direct customer service calls and can resolve issues promptly, available 24/7. This method could potentially increase sales, continuously update the customer database, and ensure security with encryption and authentication techniques for data protection. ChatGPT can help tackle a problem affecting 17% of customers, providing swift solutions and freeing customer service to focus on more complex issues.

People's Choice Award

Teylez Perez

AI Powered Chat Bot

QUIBO is an AI-powered chatbot app that interacts with retail managers to provide them with insights on sales goals performance. Managers could ask questions like "What products are selling the most right now?“, “What should we sell to reach the goal by end of day?” or "Which products are customers most likely to purchase today?" and receive instant answers based on the app's analysis of real-time data. The app might also feature a simple dashboard that displays KPIs such as sales per department, conversion rate, margin, and average ticket.
