Winner 1st Place by Adeh Mirzakhanian

Call Center Recordings AI Processor

A system that utilizes the OpenAI Wisper library to automate the transcription of call recordings from any language to any other language. Additionally, it incorporates an in-house AI model to remove personally identifiable information (PII) from both the transcribed and original versions. The system also categorizes and summarizes the conversations, assesses customer satisfaction with the service, and assigns the records to customers' history, leveraging all the capabilities of OpenAI's APIs. Furthermore, it offers solutions for call center data analysis, optimization, and advanced searching, enabling efficient and effective management of call center operations.

AI Powered Chat Bot

QUIBO is an AI-powered chatbot app that interacts with retail managers to provide them with insights on sales goals performance. Managers could ask questions like "What products are selling the most right now?“, “What should we sell to reach the goal by end of day?” or "Which products are customers most likely to purchase today?" and receive instant answers based on the app's analysis of real-time data.

The app might also feature a simple dashboard that displays KPIs such as sales per department, conversion rate, margin, and average ticket.
